Introduction

DK Books is a Website set up by Daniel Ninnis and Kristian Ninnis, brothers who have a passion for writing and reading.

DK Books gives you the chance to read the Novels and Short Stories wrote buy the website developers/writers and other writers who wish to advertise or upload their books and short stories on the DK Books.

What you should know

This guide assumes you are a member of DK Books

  • If the option is enabled the books and short stories of the web developers/authors of DK Books, and the books and short stories of website members will be linked to Amazon Kindle so the reader is able to purchase a copy of their selected choice for their Amazon kindle.
  • If the option is enabled the books and short stories of the web developers/authors of DK Books, and the books and short stories of website members will be available in hardback and paperback editions
    • forums and disscusions

    DK books offer the chance for Members to start a forum on the topic of their choice, whether it be a disscusion on the uninportance of Grammer to the Pros and cons of popular Authors. The skys the limit.

    The forums and discusions can be use to discuss problems faced on one of the many challenges DK books offer

    The forums and discussions can be used to quiz ideas for your next projects or just to chat about the last Novel you have read.

    books to read before you die

    This part of the website is part life choice, part order, part friendly opinion on as the title threatens "Books to read before you die" These books have been hand picked by the Website creators. Feel free to discuss your opinions on the choices made.

    The paperback challenge

    The paper back challenge is a great way to get involved in reading seriously. it gives you the option to try and read twelve novels in a year. More paper back challenges will be coming soon

    The paper back challenge is great way to start reading seriously or read more for those people who feel they dont read as much as they like to.

    The Short Story Challenge
    The Short Story Challenge is the same as the Paperback Challenge with only a few differences consisting of:

    The Short stories will be found easily on a electronic reading device such as Amazon Kindle

    And in most cases the reader might not recognise the authors in question, as many of the Short Story writing greats are not as mainstream as the more popular novel writers the reader is used to

    Also the Short Story World seems to be a dying craft which the Web developer/Authors of this website are eager to encourage a welcomed return.

    The Beginner writers Challenge (Short Story)

    This challenge is a great way to start writing an idea for the hobby writer just dipping his tes into the joys of writing.

    This challenge is can be a personal challenge or manuscripts can be uploaded to DK books.

    The Semi Pro's corner (Short story Writing)

    This corner is for the author with a few projects under his belt who is ready to upload more of their work

    There is no guidelines to this project and if needed be whilst writing your Short Story if you find it is turning into a novella or even a novel feel free to carry on. Fiction writing should have no bounderies. Only the boundaries of your imagination of course.

    The Seasoned Travellers Bragging Rights (Short Story Writing)

    This section is for the Writer who sees him/her selfs as a great. The instructions are simple.

    The writers Short story must consist of no more than one thousand words

    The writers Short Story should be double spaced

    The writers Short Story should be in the genre given by DK Books. (This will change monthly).

    The Beginner writers challenge (Novella Writing)

    The Beginner is challenged to write a Novella consisting of around fourteen thousand words:

  • The beginner has the chance to have fun writing a Novella.:

    • The only challenge is to write a story gently out of the begginers confort zone.
    • Before the beginner should attemp the novella the beginner should try to write a few short stories first.
    • The beginner might flow naturally from the short Story into the Novella. A short story might develop into a Novella
    • The beginner might want to read a number of Novellas before taking this challenge.
    • If the Novella pursues further feel free to create a Novel
    • There are no rules to fiction writing
  • Break the rules
    • More explanitary infomation was given to this topic to make sure the beginner is confident to pursue this challenge
    The Semi Pro's Corner (Novel writing)
    This section will probably be where the majority of the members of DK Books will be situated hence the section having its own corner.

    The Semi Pro writer will be challenged to write a novel of any genre.

    Seasoned Travellers Bragging Rights (Novel Writing)
    A Seasoned traveler will write a novel to the given guidelines:

    The Seasoned Traveler will be expected to write a novel of the genre given by DK Books. (Change of genre every six months)

    The manuscript will be to a standard a publisher will expect.

    The manuscript will be more than forty thousand words

    The Novel will be self edited to a high quality

    Competitions (monthly)

  • The guidelines are as follows:
